%label:"def:compatibleAlmostComplexSTructure"
%author:JeffHicks
%name:"compatible almost complex structure"
%type:"definition"

 
 
Let $(X,  \omega)$ be a \snip{symplectic manifold}{def:symplecticManifold} with \snip{almost complex structure}{def:almostComplexStructure} $J$.
Then we say that $J$ is a $\omega$-compatible almost complex structure if 
\[ g(v,  w)=\omega(v,  Jw)\]
is a Riemannian metric on $X$.
