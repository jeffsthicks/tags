Let $Y$ be a symplectic manifold, and let $S^{n}\subset Y$ be a Lagrangian sphere. 
We now describe a symplectomorphism called \emph{symplectic Dehn twist:}
\[\tau_{S^n}: Y\to Y,\]
described in \cite{seidel2003long}.
