Let $\mathcal C$ be a triangulated category, and $\mathcal A$ be an abelian category.
A \emph{cohomological functor} is a functor $F: \mathcal C\to \mathcal A$ which sends exact triangles 
\[A\to B\to C\to A[1]\]
to exact sequences
\[F(A)\to F(B)\to F(C).\]
