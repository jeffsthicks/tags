%name:"linear Hamiltonian"
%type:"definition"

 
 Let  $M$ be a contact manifold, and let $\RR\times M$ be its symplectization. A \emph{linear Hamiltonian} of slope $m$ is the Hamiltonian $H^m(r, x)= m\cdot \exp(r)$.
 \label{def:linearHamiltonian}
